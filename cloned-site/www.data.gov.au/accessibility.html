Accessibility

We've designed this website to meet the Australian Government's web accessibility requirements as established in the Digital Service Standard. This includes complying with legislation and standards, including the Disability Discrimination Act (1992) and Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG).

This website largely meets WCAG 2.2 Level AA.

Accessibility features

Data.gov.au is designed for access on desktops, tablets and mobile devices. You should be able to:

  • zoom in up to 400% without the text spilling off the screen
  • navigate most of the website using just a keyboard
  • listen to most of the website using a screen reader.

Report an accessibility issue

We want to provide our information for everyone, including people with a disability.

If you find anything on this site is inaccessible to you or you experience problems accessing our content, please get in touch with us via support@data.gov.au.
